Cellular Automata

Given my interests in medicine/biology and computer science, I have always been fascinated at the concept of cellular automata. Specifically, the ability of complex behaviors and patterns to emerge from simple rules. To me, this phenomenon is the perfect mixture of philosophy, biology, and CS. Some interesting studies of cellular automata in action include Conway's Game of Life and slime molds. Conway's Game has intrigued so much that I have taken up playing the ancient game of Go (due to the parallels between the two), which I strongly recommend playing as you can here.
